To find the YouTube video ID, look at the URL in the address bar when you open a video. The ID is the string after "v=" and before any "&".

For example, in the URL https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=V1ndkAyrLGI&ab_channel=OpenyourMind, the video ID is V1ndkAyrLGI.
